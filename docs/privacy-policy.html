Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Morse Comms is developed and maintained by Mitos Software Solutions. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Summary

Morse Comms is a fully offline app. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required. No data ever leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to any server
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No contacts or calendar data

Microphone Permission

The app requests access to your device's microphone solely to decode Morse code from live audio input. Audio is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded or transmitted unless you explicitly use the "Save Recording" feature. Microphone access is used only while the decoder screen is active.

Audio Recordings

If you choose to save a recording, the resulting WAV file is stored locally on your device only. The app does not upload recordings anywhere. You may share the file using your device's standard share sheet, at which point the file is handled by the app or service you choose to share it with — outside of Morse Comms' control.

Third-Party Services

Morse Comms does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry SDKs. All functionality runs entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
